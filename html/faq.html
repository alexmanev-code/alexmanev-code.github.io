Printing from your laptop can be done using this link.

Follow these steps:


  1. Choose your printing options
  2. Upload the files you wish to print
  3. Press Upload files
  4. Go to the multifunctional printer and use your HZ pass on the TouchID
  5. Make sure you have enough funds in your account
  6. Select "Print"
  7. Congratulations, you just printed your first documents in HZ!
  8. Once you are done, press "Stop" and click "Logout" in the menu

Scanning documents is completely FREE. However, you must have at least €0.07 worth of credits in your HZ pass balance in order to proceed.


  1. Register using your HZ pass on the TouchID
  2. In the TouchID menu, select the "Scan" option
  3. Follow the scanning steps
  4. Once you are done, press "Stop" on the TouchID and click "Logout"
  5. Good job, you scanned your first documents!
